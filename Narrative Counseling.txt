# Narrative Counseling Corpus

## Counseling Questions
### Questions about Hobbbies
	1. Of the subjects you are currently studying, which one do you like the most? Why?
	2. Among the assignments you've been given so far that you can remember, which one did you find most interesting to do? Why?
	3. If you could choose only three subjects to study, which would they be? Why?
	4. What do you like to do most in your free time?
	5. When you're stressed about schoolwork, what do you usually do?
	6. When you're tired from family pressures, life, or conflicts in friendships, what do you do?
	7. When you were younger, what did you enjoy doing?
	8. What hobbies do you no longer have as you grew up? When did you lose them? Why did you lose them?
	9. For friends and family's birthdays, what gifts do you usually give? Do you ever make gifts yourself to give to them?
	10. What artistic activities do you like? (For example, singing, dancing, hip-hop, drawing, sculpting, etc.)
	11. What sports activities do you enjoy? (For example, volleyball, soccer, badminton, etc.)
	12. Can you recall any moments when you were the happiest doing something? Can you describe them?
### Questions about Abilities
	1. What areas do you think you are capable in?
	2. What activities do you feel most confident participating in?
	3. What do friends and family often compliment you on?
	4. What subjects are you good or excellent in at school?
	5. What activities are you good or excellent in outside of class?
	6. Of all the assignments you've done, which type do you find the easiest?
	7. Do you think you are better at listening or at presenting?
	8. Do friends usually come to you when they are sad or when they want to go out?
	9. Are you often invited to parties and fun events?
	10. Do your friends often confide in you and ask for advice?
	11. Do you participate in extracurricular activities? If so, what are they?
	12. Besides studying, what do you usually do in your free time?
	13. Do you have any special dreams?
### Questions about Personality
	1. Do you prefer crowds or being alone more?
	2. Are you shy when going out and meeting a lot of strangers? Do you enjoy making new friends?
	3. Do you prefer attending parties with many people or just with a small group of close friends?
	4. Do you usually view things from an overall perspective or pay more attention to details?
	5. Do your friends compliment you on your ability to notice the little things?
	6. When doing assignments, do you often double-check your work and rarely make minor mistakes?
	7. Do you think you are more liberal or cautious?
	8. Do you prefer to reason things out to understand them or rely more on emotions?
	9. Do your friends describe you as being cold?
	10. When making decisions, do you tend to use emotion or reason?
	11. Do you consider yourself to be organized and punctual?
	12. Do your parents ever complain about your messiness and forgetfulness?
	13. What leadership roles do you hold in class (if any)?
### Questions about Values
	1. What is the most important thing in life to you?
	2. What is your dream? What do you want to do after you have a stable job in the future?
	3. Do you know why you were born into this life?
	4. What virtue do you think is the most important to you?
	5. What qualities do you hope for in a close friend?
	6. What qualities do you hope for in a partner?
	7. In your opinion, what does a happy life look like?
	8. In your opinion, what is an ideal job?
	9. In your life, whom do you respect the most and why?
	10. Of all the people you have met so far, whom do you love the most and why?
